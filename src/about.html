COURTYARD? LIKE THE HOTEL?
We're courtyard, a six piece indie-rock outfit from Rochester, New York. Currently, we're working on our debut, four-track, self-titled EP. We enjoy writing bops, getting noise complaints, and occasionally playing decent music.

Incidentally, we're also very popular for freelance jazz bookings amongst the RIT community. Let us know if you ever want us at your show (or banquet, ball, gala, party)!
